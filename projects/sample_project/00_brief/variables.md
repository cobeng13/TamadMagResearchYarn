# Variables

## Predictor Variables

| Variable | Type | Operational Definition | Expected Data Format | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Clustered academic grades | Independent / predictor | Academic performance grouped into curriculum or licensure-relevant clusters | Numeric cluster averages or grades | Cluster definitions, course membership, weighting, and grading scale direction must be confirmed |
| Internship or clinical performance grade | Independent / predictor | Performance during internship, clinical practicum, or related practical training | Numeric grade or rating | Availability to be confirmed |
| Pre-board grades per cluster | Independent / predictor | Performance in institutional mock board, pre-board, or board review assessment by content or subject cluster | Numeric cluster grade, score, rating, or percentage | Alignment with actual licensure exam clusters should be discussed; scoring rules and psychometric properties should be confirmed if available |
| Terminal competency or comprehensive examination results | Independent / predictor | Final institutional assessment of program-level competencies | Numeric score, rating, or pass/fail | Include only if available |
| Batch or cohort | Grouping / comparison variable | Batch, cohort, academic year, or licensure examination period used for across-batch comparison | Categorical year or batch label | Exact batch definitions and cohort years must be confirmed |
| Demographic/profile variables | Control or descriptive variables | Student characteristics such as sex, age, cohort, or admission-related data | Categorical or numeric | Use only if ethically available and analytically relevant |

## Outcome Variables

| Variable | Type | Operational Definition | Expected Data Format | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Board examination grades per cluster | Dependent / outcome | RTLE performance by board examination subject or content cluster | Numeric grade, rating, or percentage per cluster | Primary cluster-level RTLE performance outcome if available; official cluster labels must be confirmed |
| Licensure examination result | Dependent / outcome | Whether the graduate passed or failed the licensure examination | Binary categorical variable | Secondary classification outcome or primary outcome if rating is unavailable |
| Overall licensure examination rating | Dependent / outcome | Overall rating obtained in the Radiologic Technologist Licensure Examination | Numeric rating or percentage | Use if available; may support overall RTLE performance analysis |

## Possible Covariates
- Academic year or cohort
- First-time taker versus repeater status
- Time elapsed between graduation and licensure examination
- Review center attendance or structured review participation
- Admission test score or high school academic indicator, if available
- Scholarship or academic standing, if relevant and available

## Measurement Issues To Verify
- Whether higher grades indicate better or poorer performance in the institutional grading scale
- Whether clustered academic grades can be consistently computed across batches
- Whether pre-board grades per cluster are raw scores, percentages, ratings, or categorical outcomes
- Whether pre-board cluster results represent first, last, highest, or average attempt scores
- Whether pre-board item-level data, reliability, item difficulty, or item discrimination indices are available
- Whether board examination data include only first-take performance or include retakes
- Whether RTLE board examination grades per cluster are available and consistently labeled across batches
- Whether missing data are present and how they will be handled
- Whether subject-area labels match the official licensure examination structure for the relevant examination year
