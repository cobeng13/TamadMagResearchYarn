## Database-Specific Search Notes
- Google Scholar: Use broad strings first, then add Philippines or Radiologic Technology.
- PubMed: Use radiography, allied health, licensure, certification, education, and prediction terms.
- ERIC: Use academic achievement, certification, licensure, professional education, and predictive validity terms.
- Philippine E-Journals and institutional repositories: Use shorter phrase searches such as "Radiologic Technology licensure examination" and "pre-board examination licensure".
- PRC and CHED sources: Search for policy context, examination structure, outcomes, and program standards.
